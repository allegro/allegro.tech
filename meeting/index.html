allegro.tech

allegro.tech is a brand created by the best engineers in allegro to show the community who we are and what we do every day.

ATM

allegro.tech meeting a.k.a. ATM, is an annual meeting of allegro's engineers held to share knowledge about projects that we run, problems that we face and unique solutions that we implement. This is an opportunity to catch up with all the new things that happened to be difficult to communicate due to our location in various cities across Poland. ATM breaks this barrier.

“For and by IT” is the leitmotif of the event that has accompanied our efforts since the beginning, that is since 2009. This is when the concept of the conference was proposed by Marcin “Mazek” Mazurek, main coordinator of the first editions.

This two day event is organized in whole by allegro engineers.

allegro.tech meeting v8

It is our intention to have the meeting held interchangeably in one of five locations of our operations: Poznań, Warsaw, Toruń, Kraków and Wrocław. This year’s eighth edition of ATM will take place in Warsaw, on September 14th-15th, inviting our engineers to spend two days in the extraordinary surroundings of the PGE National Stadium and giving them the opportunity to listen to the presentations, lightning talks of work mates and to participate in workshops.

A new element this year is that we invite guests from outside Allegro to join us on the first day. We want to share with you everything that we find most valuable, that is the knowledge and experience of our engineers.
